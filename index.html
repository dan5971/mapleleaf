HILL MEAD BRICKWORK

Bricklayers and Brickwork Contractors in Horsham | Local Specialists for Burgess Hill, Haywards Heath and Sussex

Established in 2003 and bringing more than two decades of experience to local projects as a family-run business, Maple Leaf Brickwork offers the combined services of bricklayers, brickwork contractors, builders and garden paving specialists. Meeting the personal needs of the domestic sector in West Sussex, we trade out of Horsham and can cover all surrounding locations including the key areas of Burgess Hill and Haywards Heath to name just two.

Maple Leaf Brickwork proudly delivers high-end workmanship and is a trusted choice with new customers and those who come back to use us on a repeat basis. No job is ever too big or too small for our dependable team.

Choose us for new builds and property extensions, including the groundworks, and for the construction of retaining walls. We can also lay new driveways and patios in a choice of attractive materials including block paving. Expect a professional brand of service but with the personal touch of a friendly local business that makes you the most important person in our company.

You can contact us to arrange site visits which include free consultations and surveys, and the provision of quotations on a no-obligation basis.

Our website gallery shows examples of our previous work, and you can read what customers have to say about our services in their testimonials.
