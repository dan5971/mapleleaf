About Hill Mead Brickwork
Importantly, Hill Mead Brickwork offers an in-house service. We retain total control over your project, managing it as a single team so that you only ever need to deal with one company and one voice. With the required liability insurances in place, backed by 12-month workmanship guarantees, we hire the services of local bricklayers and brickwork contractors who have earned their NVQ qualifications and show genuine pride and passion for the job.

Brickwork is our specialist skillset, but we can offer the wider service range of general builders and manage your project from concept to completion.
Our services include:
Bricklaying

The construction of brickwork for new builds, extensions and garden walls, to include the replacement of chipped or split bricks and repointing mortar.

Retaining Walls

With this service, we build lateral walls with rigid structures to support soil in a way the keeps it safely retained at separate levels over two different sides.

Groundworks

The groundworks phase of a project includes site clearance, bulk excavation and the installation of drainage with foundations poured up to the DPC.

New Driveways

Gain a safe offroad parking space with one of our new driveways to reduce insurance premiums and reduce instances of damage to family vehicles.

Paving and Patios

Paving work comes naturally to bricklayers and brickwork contractors, so you can trust us to lay concrete flagstones, block paving and natural stone tiles.

Property Extensions

If you plan to increase the footprint of a home to gain more habitable space, we can construct property extensions in a choice of functional designs.

At Hill Mead Brickwork, we maintain exceptional standards of workmanship by treating every property as if it were our own. Because our team consists of time-served bricklayers and brickwork contractors, you can expect high-end finishes on all projects undertaken without having to hire additional services from other companies at the cost of your personal time and your finances.

Our reputation in Horsham and the surrounding Burgess Hill, Haywards Heath and West Sussex areas is one that we work on tirelessly to keep intact.
